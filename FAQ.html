Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Complimenting

On here, complimenting is writing something nice about a person you appreciate for what they do. It can be anyone, as long as they have a Minecraft username to go by.

Where Can I Compliment

You can compliment someone by going here!

My Compliment Isn't Showing Up

I usually get 5-10 submissions of compliments a day (as of now), and most submissions have more than one compliment attatched to them; so depending on all of these factors, and the fact that I have school 75% of the day (not including weekends), it may take some time for me to update compliments. The only other reason a compliment was not posted is because the compliment was not suitable for public view (this includes inappropriate language, threats, or anything explicit), or it wasn't a real compliment. What makes a compliment a compliment is the fact that the user that is submitting the compliment is listing at least one thing they like or admire of the user they are complimenting.

I've Changed My Minecraft Username

Currently, there isn't an official way to change your page name and compliment username, since this is a rare occurance. However, with the growing "population" of users, this could become a regular occurance. If you do happen to change your username, please contact me at support@glitchedcode.me. Make sure to include your previous username, UUID, your new username, and proof that you own the account. This can be as simple as a screenshot of you signed in on the Minecraft program. Sometime in the near future I will probably have it so you can just submit another form, but for now this is what I have. Sorry for any inconvenience this brings.

What Do I Get When I Donate

Currently, there aren't that many things I can give for donating. If you'd like to like to suggest some ideas go ahead and send an e-mail to support@glitchedcode.me.

Are There Any Rules

There will be an official rules page soon, but for now you can go off of these:

  • Don't submit anything you wouldn't on a public website.

  • Don't abuse the form and submit a lot of submissions in a short time.

  • Don't submit anything explicit. This includes inappropriate language (discluding words like, "ass" and "damn") and NSFW content.
